How we would look at it.

Eduba looks at a business like this the way we look at any ops-heavy operator. 60 percent of what eats your week is traditional code and database work: a working site, a product database, an order form, a payment rail. 30 percent is rule-based: device-by-device FDA status, per-clinic financing terms, per-state shipping rules. Ten percent is genuine AI: the assistant that answers a clinic owner at 9 p.m. with the same answer Lior would give, and books the demo.

Most distributors in your category pay agencies to rebuild the whole stack as if it were all AI. That is why the projects cost eighty thousand and ship late. We would not do that to you.
